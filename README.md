# SmartYouth Health Assistant (SYHA)
### Umufasha w'Ubuzima ku Rubyiruko

> An AI-powered digital platform improving access to Sexual & Reproductive Health (SRH) and Mental Health support for young people in Rwanda.

---

## 🌍 Overview

**SYHA** provides a safe, anonymous, and stigma-free environment where youth aged 15–30 can access reliable health information and ask sensitive questions confidentially — in **English or Kinyarwanda**.

Built as part of the **iAccelerator Phase 7** application, addressing two key challenges:
- 🩺 Sexual & Reproductive Health (SRH), HIV prevention, and menstrual health
- 🧠 Mental health awareness and inclusive community wellbeing

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🌐 Bilingual | Full English & Kinyarwanda language support |
| 💬 Anonymous Q&A | Ask sensitive health questions without fear |
| 🧠 Mental Health | Stress, anxiety, and depression awareness |
| ❤️ SRH Education | HIV prevention, menstrual health, reproductive health |
| ♿ Accessibility | Designed with inclusion for persons with disabilities |
